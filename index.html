About Vivo Distro

The Heartbeat of Entertainment

Vivo Distro is a multi-purpose digital platform built to connect performers, agents, and businesses in one powerful online space.

We provide performers with a professional digital presence where they can showcase their talent, manage their profiles, and be discovered by the right people. For agents and managers, Vivo Distro works as an efficient digital management tool — making it easier to organize talent, promote artists, and connect with opportunities.

For businesses and event organizers, the platform doubles as an advertising and talent discovery hub. Companies can promote events, search for performers, and book talent directly through a streamlined, modern system designed for the entertainment industry.

Vivo Distro brings together talent management, digital promotion, and event advertising into one connected platform — simplifying how the industry discovers, manages, and books performers in the digital age.

One platform. Three industries. Endless opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower South African talent by providing a professional platform that connects performers with opportunities, simplifies management for agents, and helps bookers find the perfect talent for any event.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading entertainment technology platform, revolutionizing how the industry discovers, manages, and books talent across the continent.
